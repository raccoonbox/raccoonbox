Welcome to Graphic Design 101

What is Graphic Design?
Graphic design is the art of combining text, images, and ideas to create visually appealing content. It’s used in everything from advertisements and websites to logos and packaging.

Why Graphic Design Matters
Good design grabs attention, conveys information quickly, and creates a memorable experience. It’s essential for effective communication in both digital and print media.

Key Elements of Graphic Design

Typography: Choosing fonts and arranging text to be both readable and visually appealing.

Color: Using colors to evoke emotions and create a cohesive look.
Images: Incorporating photos, illustrations, and icons to enhance the message.
Layout: Organizing all elements harmoniously to guide the viewer’s eye.
