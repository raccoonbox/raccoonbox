Portrait photography

By Raccoonbox Production
14 April 2023

Good photography is artistic expression, capturing the history and meaningful communication. That is what Raccoonbox Photography is all about.

Photography allows us to capture and preserve important moments in our lives, such as weddings, births, and other special occasions. These images serve as powerful reminders of the people and events that are most important to us.

Trusted Raccoonbox photographers:
Mikko Kurvi - instagram.com/chili.red.dot - Studio and fashion photographer.
Konstantin Nikkari - instagram.com/konstantin.nikkari - Documentary photographer.
Alessandro Rampazzo - alessandrorampazzo.com - Journalist and event photographer.
Jaana Vuola - jaanavuola.com - Animal and portrait photographer.

Photography is art. It allows individuals to express themselves creatively. We use composition, lighting, and other techniques to create images that are aesthetically pleasing and thought-provoking.

Photographs are important historical documents that provide a visual record of significant events, people, and places. Photos tell stories, raise awareness of social issues, and promote products or services.

Lets make good photos! 📸
